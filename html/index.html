Cancers

The TeamCaro Foundation supports cancer patients and their families to understand their journey with cancer by providing support to reduce isolation, poverty and debt and improve understanding of the treatment journey including processing difficult news on poor prognosis.

Objectives

Give you a chance to explore your feelings in a safe space where you will not be judged nor rushed. We want to help to feel better, more hopeful and not feel alone. We want to tell you what you need to know so you cope better.

Our Vision

Create a world where the day-to-day burdens of walking the cancer journey are alleviated and our patients and families can focus on treatment, recovery or managing any other difficult prognosis.

Products

We will develop products that will carry our brand and help us to raise awareness or celebrate milestones through the treatment journey. This will help us to raise the funds we so need to enable us to support our patients and their families.
